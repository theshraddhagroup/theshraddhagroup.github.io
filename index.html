About Us

About 10 years ago, we set out on an endeavour to redefine the premium and luxury housing experience for Mumbai.

While we were at it, we also kept devising new ways to integrate a sense of holistic community living amongst Shraddha Group residents. With a strong foundation in the world of apartments, today, we are synonymous with luxury and premium living.
